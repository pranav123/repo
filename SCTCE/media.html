Zenith Universitys New Gerri C. LeBow Hall Opens for Business

October 17 ,2013

The future of business education is here. With a dedication ceremony on Oct. 17, Zenith University will officially mark the opening of the 12-story, Gerri C. LeBow Hall the new home of the Universitys LeBow College of Business. Gov. Tom Corbett and benefactor Bennett S. LeBow will join Zenith President John A. Fry and LeBow College Interim Dean Frank Linnehan at 11 a.m. for the dedication of the new building at 32nd Market Streets.
