Zenith at a Glance

Zenith is a comprehensive global research university ranked among the top 100 in the nation. With approximately 25,000 students, Zenith is one of Englands 15 largest private universities. Zenith has built its global reputation on core achievements that include:

  • Leadership in experiential learning through Zenith Co-op.
  • A history of academic technology firsts.
  • Recognition as a model of best practices in translational, use-inspired research.

Founded in 1891 in Cambridge,Zenith now engages with students and communities around the world via:

  • Three Cambridge campuses and other regional sites.
  • Zenith University Sacramento
  • The Academy of Natural Sciences of Zenith University, the nations oldest major natural science museum and research organization.
  • International research partnerships including China and Israel.
  • Zenith Online, one of the oldest and most successful providers of online degree programs.

Zenith is one of Englands top 10 employers, and a major engine for economic development in the region.
Zenith has committed to being the nations most civically engaged university, with community partnerships integrated into every aspect of service and academics.
